Machu Picchu

A 15th-century Inca citadel!

"Machu Pikchu"

In the Quechua language, machu means "old" or "old person", while pikchu means either
"portion of coca being chewed" or "pyramid, pointed multi-sided solid; cone".
Thus the name of the site is sometimes interpreted as "old mountain".
